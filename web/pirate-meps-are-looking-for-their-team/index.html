Pirate MEPs are looking for their team!

2019-07-01

Join the Pirates in the European Parliament! They are opening several positions and more are to come. For now there are four positions opened - and feel free to contact the MEPs in case you feel like you can contribute them in their tasks, even if you don't see a position relevant to you opened yet. Also be prepared for internships: the best way to gain valuable experience and insight into real (and Pirate) politics.

Positions:

  • Administrative assistant
  • Head of Office
  • Policy Advisor (INTA, AFET, SEDE)
  • Policy Assistant (INTA, AFET, SEDE)
  • Policy Advisors (various committees)
  • IT Administrator
  • Communications trainee (Brussels/Berlin)

To apply please follow instructions in the links. We are looking forward to hear from you!
