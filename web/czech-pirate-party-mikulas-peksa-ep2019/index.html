Czech Pirate Party – Mikuláš Peksa – EP2019

2019-04-27

For the upcoming EU elections we will interview Pirate Party top candidates - and current Pirate member of the European Parliament, Julia Reda - in short 10-15 minute audio/video interviews (playlist here). Get to know the Czech Pirate Party#3 – Mikuláš Peksa. Mikuláš is a deputy and vice-chairman of the Czech Pirate Party and the European Pirate Party. He also serves as vice-chairman of the House Affairs Committee of the European Union and the guarantor of the Pirates Foreign Team. In the interview they discuss the upcoming elections and how to proceed with EU law, such as the new copyright directive
